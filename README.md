# Amazon Sales Intelligence Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** designed to analyze Amazon product sales and customer feedback data. The dashboard provides actionable insights into product categories, pricing segments, discounts, ratings, and overall product performance, enabling data-driven business decisions.

##  Objectives
- Analyze product distribution across categories.
- Identify pricing trends using price buckets.
- Evaluate customer ratings and product quality.
- Measure discount patterns across categories.
- Support data-driven business decisions through visual analytics.

##  Dashboard Features

###  Category Market Share

Visualizes the distribution of products across major categories:

- Electronics
- Computers & Accessories
- Home & Kitchen
- Office Products
- Other Categories
### Price Bucket Analysis

Products are segmented into the following price ranges:

- Under ₹500
- ₹500 – ₹1K
- ₹1K – ₹2K
- ₹2K – ₹5K
- Premium ₹5K+

This helps identify pricing trends and customer purchasing patterns.

###  Rating Health Indicator

Provides a quick overview of customer satisfaction using average product ratings and quality indicators.

### Discount Analysis

Analyzes average discount percentages across product categories to uncover promotional strategies and pricing behavior.

###  Category vs Price Matrix

Displays product counts across different categories and pricing segments, helping users identify category-wise price distribution.

## Business Insights

- Electronics and Computers & Accessories dominate the product portfolio.
- Most products fall within the ₹500–₹2,000 price range.
- Customer ratings indicate generally high satisfaction levels.
- Discount strategies vary significantly across product categories.
- Price segmentation helps identify premium and budget-focused categories.
